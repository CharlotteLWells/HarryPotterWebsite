Gryffindor

Gryffindor values bravery, daring, nerve and chivalry. Its symbol is the lion and its colours are scarlet and gold. Professor McGonagall was the most recent Head of Gryffindor. Sir Nicholas de Mimsy-Porpington, also known as "Nearly Headless Nick", was the house ghost. The founder of the house was Godric Gryffindor. Gryffindor relates to the element of Fire. The dormitary and common room was located in one of the highest towers at Hogwarts, the entrance was situated on the seventh floor in the east wing of the castle and is guarded by a portrait of The Fat Lady. She will only give entrance if given the correct password, which changes every year.

Godric Gryffindor

Professor Godric Gryffindor was a wizard of Medieval times and one of the Four Founders of Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry. He was great friends with Salazar Slytherin but disliked him later when Salazar believed that they should not accept Muggle-born students at Hogwarts. It is unknown when he died. Gryffindor's birthplace became known was Godrics Hollow, in his honour. Godric Gryffindor's portrait still remains at Hogwarts.

The Gryffindor sword and the Sorting Hat

Godric left a goblin-made sword (known as 'The Sword of Gryffindor') covered in rubies and the Sorting Hat, these two items shared a bond where only a 'true gryffindor' can ask the sword to come out of the hat.
