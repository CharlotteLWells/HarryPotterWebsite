Harry Potter

Harry Potter is a half-blood wizard that first lives in the muggle world (non-magic) and discovers his parents went to Hogwarts. He is the first one who has defeated you know who (Voldemort) and through the his school years he stops evil trying to take over Hogwarts and the world with the help of his two friends, Ron and Hermione.

Hermione Granger

Hermione Granger and her parents are muggles but she is accepted into Hogwarts because of her intelligence and love for magic. She is the brightest student in her year and becomes friends with Harry and Ron.

Ron Weasley

Ron Weasley and his family are pure-blood he has one sister and five brothers, Ron is the youngest boy. Both his parents went to Hogwarts, he becomes friends with Harry and Hermione, even though he gets easily scared he stays by his friend's sides.
