Magical creatures are a colourful aspect of the wizarding world in the Harry Potter series by J. K. Rowling. Throughout the seven books of the series, Harry and his friends come across many of these creatures on their adventures, as well as in the Care of Magical Creatures class at Hogwarts. Some of these creatures are friendly, however some can be very dangerous to wizards and witches.
